What kind of dog is Arrow?

The short answer is - the best kind! Though if you want to get more specific, Arrow is a mixture of over 8 different dog breeds. The top breed contributor to Arrow's genetic makeup is Chihuahua. As you can see from the genetic mapping below, that's only one small part of what makes Arrow, well, Arrow.

How old is Arrow?

Arrow is an eternal being who has existed for all of time and who will continue to exist for the rest of time.

Where did he get those ears?

Current hypotheses are that he is a relative of one of the following:

  • Fennec fox
  • Long-eared jerboa
  • California leaf-nosed bat
  • Grey long eared bat
  • Jackrabbit
  • Free elf

Can I get my own Arrow art?

Arrow art is not available to the general public at this time. If you would like to use any images featuring the dapper doggo, feel free to email your request to Arrow's representatives. Please include information about what the images will be used for.

Can Arrow come to my event?

While Arrow would love to attend your event, he is not currently booking appearances due to a very busy schedule. His current, walk, nap, play, eat, sleep routine is highly taxing and fills up nearly every minute of his days. This section will be updated once openings are available on Arrow's calendar.
